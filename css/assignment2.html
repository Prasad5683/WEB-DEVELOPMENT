Explore the Houses of Hogwarts:

  • Gryffindor: Known for bravery, chivalry, daring, nerve, and determination.
  • Hufflepuff: Values hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play.
  • Ravenclaw: Celebrates intelligence, learning, wisdom, wit, and creativity.
  • Slytherin: Emphasizes ambition, cunning, leadership, resourcefulness, and self-preservation.
